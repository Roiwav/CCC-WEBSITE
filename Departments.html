The City College of Calamba (CCC) is dedicated to providing accessible,high-quality education that empowers students and enhances our community. With focused departments in Arts and Sciences, Business and Accountancy, Computing and Informatics, and Teacher Education, CCC offers a well-rounded academic experience aimed at equipping students with the necessary skills, knowledge, and real-world experience for success in their selected careers. Our college cultivates a nurturing and dynamic atmosphere that promotes lifelong learning, creativity, and service to the community.
